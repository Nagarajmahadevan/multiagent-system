Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Verd ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use getverd.com. By using Verd, you agree to the practices described here.

What we collect

  • Account data: Your email address and profile name as provided by Google when you sign in.
  • Conversation data: The questions you ask Verd and the responses generated by our AI agents.
  • Usage data: Token counts, costs per query, and timing for billing and analytics.
  • Payment metadata: Transaction IDs and amounts (we do not store card numbers; payments are processed by our PSP).
  • Technical data: IP address (for fraud prevention and rate-limiting only — not retained beyond 30 days).

How we use your data

  • To provide the AI debate service and manage your credit balance.
  • To improve product quality and debug issues.
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, account alerts). We do not send marketing emails unless you opt in.
  • To detect and prevent abuse.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your conversations to train our own models.

Third parties we share data with

  • Google — sign-in / authentication
  • Supabase — database hosting (account + conversations)
  • Razorpay — payment processing
  • AI model providers — your query content is forwarded to one or more of: DeepSeek, Google (Gemini), OpenAI (GPT), Anthropic (Claude). Each provider has its own privacy policy; we don't control their handling.
  • Hosting providers — server infrastructure (Railway, Cloudflare).

Data retention

Your account and conversation history are retained while your account is active. Deleting your account permanently removes your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., tax records of past payments).

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and associated data (use the "Delete my account" option in the recharge screen, or email us).
  • Export your conversation history (email us — we'll send a JSON export within 14 days).

EU/UK users have additional rights under the GDPR; California users under the CCPA. Email support@getverd.com to exercise any right.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and payment session state. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. EU/UK visitors see a consent banner on their first visit.

Children

Verd is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will notify active users by email. The "Last updated" date above always reflects the current version.

Contact

For any privacy question or data request, email support@getverd.com.
